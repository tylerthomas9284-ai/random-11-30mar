About Homestead Aura

Your trusted partner for professional home decor and interior styling in San Antonio

Who We Are

Homestead Aura is a professional home decor service provider dedicated to transforming houses into beautiful, personalized homes. Based in San Antonio, TX, we specialize in interior styling, color consultation, furniture arrangement, and complete room makeovers.

Our team of experienced designers works closely with homeowners to understand their unique style preferences, lifestyle needs, and budget considerations. We believe that great design should be accessible and that every home deserves to be both beautiful and functional.

Whether you're looking to refresh a single room, stage your home for sale, or undertake a complete interior transformation, we bring creativity, expertise, and attention to detail to every project.
